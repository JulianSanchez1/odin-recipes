Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Description

If you love pasta then this easy garlic shrimp pasta is going to be your go-to pasta dish because it is not only delicious, but it’s really easy to make and doesn’t need a billion ingredients. Shrimp cooks quickly which makes this pasta dish the ideal meal to make when you don’t have much time to get dinner on the table. Made with sauteed shrimp and tossed in with a garlic tomato parmesan sauce. If you don’t like shrimp you may swap it for any other protein of choice.

ingredients

  • Spaghetti:use any of your favorite pasta.
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Shrimp
  • Chili Flakes
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook pasta, reserving one cup of pasta water to add to the sauce, if necessary.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, season (per recipe below) and cook the shrimp. Cook just until pink, it’ll cook a bit more in the sauce so be sure you do not overcook.
  3. Remove and deglaze pan with white wine and lemon juice.
  4. Add remaining ingredients, stirring constantly until sauce is barely thickened.
  5. Add shrimp and pasta back to the pot and add the parsley. Continue stirring until heated thoroughly.
